About Me

I am a second-degree computer science student at UBC in the BCS program. A few years ago, while working with the Vancouver Police Department, I began learning programming in my spare time and immediately got hooked. Coupled with my interest in cybercrime from my days studying criminology, I decided to make the jump and pursue CS. Since then, it has been an exciting whirlwind of hackathons, programming projects, and work experience.

When I'm not busy tapping away at the keys, you can most likely find me sleeping hiking one of the local trails, making a mean pasta carbonara, or chalking up yet another win in Mario Kart 64.

My other love in life is travelling. I have been lucky enough to visit 24 countries and live in 3 of them!

Austria, Belgium, Cambodia, Denmark, France, Germany, Hong Kong, Hungary, Ireland, Italy, Japan, Netherlands, Poland, Slovakia, Sweden, Switzerland, Taiwan, Thailand, the United Kingdom, the United States, Vatican City, Vietnam
